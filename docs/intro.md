# Introduction to Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics

Welcome to the comprehensive textbook on Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics. This guide covers the fundamental concepts, advanced techniques, and practical applications of physical artificial intelligence and humanoid robotics.

## What You'll Learn

This textbook is designed to take you through a complete journey from understanding the basics of physical AI to developing sophisticated humanoid robots. We'll explore:

- Foundations of Physical AI
- Robotic nervous systems and control
- Digital twins and simulation environments
- AI perception and control systems
- Vision-language-action frameworks
- Humanoid robot development
- Conversational robotics
- Hardware requirements and platforms

## Course Overview

The content is organized into progressive modules that build upon each other, allowing you to develop a deep understanding of the field step by step.

## Getting Started

Begin with Module 0 to establish a solid foundation, then progress through each module as your understanding grows.
